About Us

Welcome to our slice of Italy, where passion for food meets the art of tradition. Our journey to America has not been easy, but it has been incredibly rewarding. When we first set foot on American soil, we were determined to share the authentic side of Italy with this beautiful country.

Our founders embarked on this adventure with a dream - a dream to bring the taste of Italy's charming streets and cozy trattorias to the heart of America. It was a journey filled with challenges, from navigating new cultures to sourcing the finest ingredients. But with unwavering dedication, we persevered.

Today, as we serve you the flavors of Italy, we take immense pride in knowing that we've succeeded in our mission. We've witnessed the joy on your faces as you savor each bite of our handmade pasta, wood-fired pizzas, and delightful desserts. It's this joy, this appreciation for the authentic, that fuels our passion every day.

So, we invite you to embark on your own culinary journey and visit us at our location nestled in the heart of one of the most vibrant and diverse student communities in North Carolina, UNCG. Our restaurant is not just a place to dine; it's a destination where you can experience the true essence of Italian cuisine, right here in your own backyard.
